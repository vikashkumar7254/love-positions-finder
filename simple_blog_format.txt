## Story Content

**Kahaani: Pehli Raat, Pehla Ehsaas** 🌙

Sana aur Arjun shaadi ke saare rasmon ke baad jab kamre mein aaye, hawa mein itni si mehak thi—**gulab aur sandalwood ki**. Dil tez dhadak raha tha, par aankhon mein sukoon tha. Room ki roshni halki, soft music chalu, fairy lights ke beech Sana ne apna dupatta halka sa sambhala. Arjun ne uske haath thaame aur kaha, *"Aaj se har darr mera, har khushi tumhari."* Sana ki palkein jhuk gayi; usne apna sir Arjun ke kandhe par rakh diya. Us pal mein waqt ruk sa gaya—**touch soft, nazdeekiyan warm, aur har baat pe respect first**. Jo lafzon se na ho saka, woh aankhon ne keh diya. 💞

Thodi si masti, thodi si sharm, aur bohot sa pyaar—Arjun ne Sana ko ek chhota sa note diya: *"You are my home."* Sana ne reply mein uske haath par ek chhota sa heart bana diya. Raat lamhi-ban-lamha khilti rahi—har kiss mein reassurance, har hug mein safety. **Sensuality yahin se banti hai—jab consent, comfort aur connection saath ho.** 🔥✨
